Coffee Spritz

A coffee spritz, who would have thought of adding tonic and citrus to your morning cup of brown?Well who ever did, I love you. The citrus notes and tonic paired with a complementary coffee(or just your regular mocha, no one cares - trust us) is amazing.

In this recipe we use freshly squeezed grapefruit juice as our citrus pairing. Lemon and orange are also refreshing alternatives, though keep in mind it is the citrus acidity you're looking to pair with the coffee.

We add a simple syrup to give it a sweet boost and you can infuse that with ANYTHING! Literally anything! Our suggestions are vanilla, cardamon and ginger. A simple sugar syrup is deceptively easy and you can store it in the fridge when you make a little extra. 1 part sugar, to 1 part water. Whilst the sugar disolves you can add a small amount of flavouring to taste.

Time: 5 mins | Infusing for sugar syrup: 1hr 30 mins | Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • 1/2 ounce simple sugar syrup
  • 1 ounce of espresso
  • 2 ounces of fresh grapefruit juice
  • tonic water(or soda)
  • ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Combine simple syrup, coffee and grapefruit juice in a tall glass full of ice, and top with tonic water. Give it a swizzle!

    Notes:

  • We find everyone is different with their coffee to citrus ratios so give it a taste test and go wild.
