What is TTotal all about?

Do you love being alcohol free? So do we! And we're proud of it!

TTotal is our way of representing teetotalism. Teetotalism is the practice or promotion of complete personal abstinence from alcoholic beverages. A person who practises teetotalism is called a teetotaler or is simply said to be teetotal.

We don't want to abstain from our friends and society who do love a drink, and so we're providing links and places to go who support our TTotal movement of providing quality non alcoholic beverages.

'A simple, fun and comphrensive approach that aims to reduce the level of alcohol-related harm and ill-health in Melboure.'

We work with our community to find ways to keep socialising without the impacts alcohol related harm.
