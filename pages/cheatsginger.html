Cheats Ginger No Alcohol Beer

Create a wonderfully refreshing alcohol-free summer spritz with only four ingredients - make as spicy as you like. We're talking'ginger' spice, not chilli spice. Though, if you like that extra kick, chuck a small amount of fresh chilli in the batch.

This is a no fermentation ginger beer, our cheats way! Rather in this recipe we're creating more of a syrup to add to lemonade or soda.

Prep: 10 mins | No Cook | Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 100g ginger, roughly chopped
  • 1 lemon, chopped(peel and pith discarded)
  • 100g light muscovado sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 1 L bottle of lemonade or soda to serve.
  • Mint to garnish

Instructions

  1. Put the ginger and lemon in a bowl, pour over 300ml water and blitz with a hand blender until very smooth. Strain through a sieve into a bowl, then press the pulp to remove as much liquid as you can. Add the sugar to the bowl and blitz again.
  2. Decant into a bottle. Chill until ready to use. Add the lemonade or sparkling water to dilute before serving.

    Notes:

  • As there is no fermentation process in this recipe there will be no alcohol present.
