No Alcohol Brands to look out for

Have you heard? There is a growing trend around town for no (or low) alcohol beverages. Here at TTotal HQ are working with producers of fine products to ensure they have options for us Teetotalers. We love it!

We've compiled a selection of stockists in the North, South and West to find your favourite no (or low) alcohol brands. Click through to find the Australian brands killing it with tasty

Stockists

Beer

Sobah

Sobah Beverages are an Australian Aboriginal and family run company making a range of premium non-alcoholic craft beers infused with native fruit and spices. sobah.com.au

Heaps Normal

The Heaps Normal Quiet XPA tastes like a delicious, refreshing beer. Tropical and citrus aroma with a lingering and unmistakably beery finish. heapsnormal.com

Nort

Golden in colour with a fruity aroma and crisp, clean finish. NORT Refreshing Ale is a thirst quenching beer that you can swim, parent, exercise, work and drive after. nort.beer

Wine

Billabong Red

A fuller bodied de-alcoholised red wine with light oak characters. Patritti Wines have been producing a range of fine, de-alcoholised wines since 1995.

TÖST

Tost non-alcoholic beverages are crafted to be as meaningful and sophisticated as alcoholic beverages but without the alcohol. Lower calorie, refreshingly dry and not too sweet. tostbeverages.com

Maggie Beer

A non-alcoholic wine made from the juice of Chardonnay grapes, Maggie Beer Sparkling Chardonnay has the bead of Champagne and the mouth feel of a good wine. maggiebeer.com.au

Spirits

Lyre's

This unique spirit has been impossibly crafted to capture the essence of a gently mellowed American Classic Bourbon Malt with flavours that are distinct and contemporary. lyres.com.au

Brunswick Aces

Sapiir was the first thing we perfected in our stills, an expertly crafted range of non-alcoholic, gin inspired, botanical alternatives to alcohol. brunswickaces.com

Seedlip

The original distilled non-alcoholic spirits Seedlip Grove Taste 42 is sophisticated, bright, citrus blend of Mediterranean Orange, Lemon Peel, Lemongrass and Ginger with a dry finish. seedlipdrinks.com

Stockists

Carwyn Cellars

Thornbury locals for 11 years. Proud to be completely independent, and we support small producers of craft beer, wine, and spirits. You'll find hard to find and globally renowned cellar worthy ales. carwyncellars.com.au

Blackhearts & Sparrows

Blackhearts & Sparrows started out as a home for boutique and obscure wines but has increasingly become one of the best places to unearth good beer. website

MR West

Both bottleshop and bar upstairs, this is truly a House of Hops & Fine Drops. Shop Craft Beer, Fine Wine and Spirits. One of the best bottle shops in Melbourne. mrwest.com.au
