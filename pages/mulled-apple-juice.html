Mulled Apple Juice

Mulled apple juice is an oldy, but a goodie for full family and campfire fun, without the alcohol! So many senses are activated during the cooking and tasting, it smells amazing and tastes amazing!

We've given you the basics and spice ratios, but really it's up to you on balace of flavour. Do you like it more tart or sweet? And remember, fresh is best but if you don't have the time to individually squeeze each apple, store bought is a-okay too.

What could be more festive and warming than a mulled bevvie? This mulled apple juice is full of the spices of Christmas(in July for us Aussies) and will warm you up after a long day.

Prep: 5 mins | Cook: 10 mins | Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 L fresh apple juice
  • peel of one orange
  • 2 cinnamon stick
  • 5 cloves
  • 2 cardamon pods
  • sugar or honey, to taste
  • grated nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Simmer the apple juice with the strips of orange peel and all the spices for about 5-10 mins until all the flavours have infused. Sweeten to taste.
  2. Serve each drink with a little orange peel and a piece of cinnamon stick, if you like.

    Notes:

  • As there is no alcohol in this recipe there is no need to boil it for ages to cook the alcohol off.
  • We love adding more citrus. Add the juice and the peel of a lemon. You can add the juice of the orange. Make it your own, but try not to include the white pith of the peel as it can add bitterness.
